Tempered yogurt

Description

Hearty yogurt dish tempered with fat, spices and herbs

Ingredients

  • Plain greek yogurt- 250 Ml
  • Tumeric- 1/4 Tbs
  • Onions- 250 Gms
  • Fresh thai chillies- 3 Nos
  • Garlic- 4 Cloves
  • Fresh curry leaves- 1/2 Handful
  • Tempering spices
    • Mustard seeds 1/2 tbs
    • Cummin seeds 1/2 tbs
    • Chana Dal 1/2 tbs
    • Moong Dal 1/2 tbs

Steps

  1. Roughly mash the chillies, garlic and onions in a mortar and pestel
  2. In a bowl add in the yogurt and beat it with a whisk until smooth
  3. Add oil into a pan and wait until its hot
  4. Add in the tempering spices and sautee until fragrant
  5. Add tumeric and sautee for 30 seconds
  6. Add the roughly mashed onions, garlic and chillies to the pan
  7. Sautee the until onions turn translucent
  8. Turn off the heat and add half a handful of curry leaves and sautee
  9. Add the tempered oil to the yogurt bowl and mix until completly incorporated and enjoy!!
