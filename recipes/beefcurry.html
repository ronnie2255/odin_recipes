Beef Curry

Description

Spicy beef curry cooked in duck fat, onions, garlic and slow cooked with a wide selection of spices

Ingredients

  • Beef Chuck- 1 Kg
  • Duck Fat- 3 Tbs
  • Onions- 250 Gms
  • Tomatoes- 250 Gms
  • Garlic- 5 Cloves
  • Ginger- 1/2 inch
  • Tomato Paste- 2 Tbs
  • Tumeric- 1/4 Tbs
  • Chilli Powder- 1 Tbs
  • Corainder Powder- 2 Tbs
  • Garam Masala- 1 Tbs
  • Heavy Bottomed Pan- any deep bottom pan will work
  • Patience- Minimum 3 Hrs (lmao)

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan and add Duck Fat and wait until its hot
  2. Add your thinly sliced garlic & ginger, sautee until raw fragrance is gone dont let them burn!!
  3. Add your sliced Onions and sautee them until translucent
  4. Add tomatoe paste and sautee until its incorporated well with the Onions
  5. Add Tumeric, Chilli, Coriander & Garam Masala, sautee them for a minute
  6. Add the Beef chuck cut into 1/2 inch cubes and increase the burner temperature to Max
  7. Continuously sautee the beef until it starts to release its water and that water is dried out
  8. Add 500 ml of water and salt to taste and stir well
  9. Reduce the temperature to medium-low and close the lid
  10. Cook for a minimum of 3 Hrs stirring ocassionally and adding water to get to the desired consitency
