Easiest tomato recipe

Description

The easiest curry that will blow your mind!! pair it with naan, chapathi or rice and its just perfect

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes- 1 Kg
  • Onions- 250 Gms
  • Fresh thai chillies- 3 Nos
  • Ginger-Garlic paste- 1 1/2 Tbs
  • Tumeric- 1/4 Tbs
  • Chilli Powder- 1 Tbs
  • Any kind of oil- 2Tbs

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan, add onions and sautee them until translucent
  2. Add in the spices and sautee them for a minute
  3. Add salt to taste
  4. Add the tomatoes and cook until mushy
  5. Top it it with fresh coriander leaves and enjoy!!
